# Suno v5 Style Definitions

## Arrangement Styles

### lofi
- **Core**: dusty vinyl texture, tape saturation, low-pass warmth, mellow Rhodes/EP
- **Drums**: lazy boom-bap, subtle swing, sidechained kick
- **Texture**: vinyl crackle, tape hiss, analog warmth, bitcrushed shimmer
- **Tempo**: 70-90 BPM
- **Avoid**: bright synths, hard-hitting drums, aggressive bass drops

### piano
- **Core**: felt/upright piano melody + harmony, expressive dynamics
- **Support**: soft pad, subtle reverb, light pedal resonance
- **Texture**: intimate, warm, acoustic room feel
- **Tempo**: 80-120 BPM
- **Avoid**: heavy EDM, distorted synths, vocal chops

### musicbox
- **Core**: music box leads melody, bell-like sparkle
- **Support**: soft pads, gentle piano, glockenspiel accents
- **Texture**: cute, cozy, crystalline, magical
- **Tempo**: 70-110 BPM
- **Avoid**: big drums, trap hats, EDM risers

### piano_musicbox
- **Core**: music box melody + upright piano chords/counter-melody
- **Support**: soft strings pad, glockenspiel accents
- **Texture**: heartwarming, intimate duet of timbres
- **Tempo**: 75-115 BPM
- **Avoid**: over-busy orchestration, heavy percussion

## Mood Modifiers

| Modifier | Musical Translation |
|----------|-------------------|
| cozy | warm reverb, close-mic feel, gentle dynamics, safe |
| dreamy | soft-focus, lush reverb, slow attack pads, ethereal |
| bittersweet | minor-to-major shifts, gentle melancholy, nostalgic longing |
| uplifting | bright major key, light bounce, hopeful progression |
| mysterious | chromatic hints, sparse arrangement, subtle tension, dim |
| rainy | patter-like percussion, watercolor pads, grey-sky melancholy |
| night | deep bass warmth, sparse high notes, quiet intimacy, moonlit |
| nostalgic | warm analog texture, simple melody, childhood memory feel |
| epic | layered build, wide stereo, cinematic swell (use sparingly) |
| ambient | minimal melody, lots of space, slow evolution, textural |

## Musical Parameters

### BPM Guidelines
- Ambient/Dreamy: 60-75
- Lofi/Chill: 70-90
- Piano ballad: 80-110
- Music box: 70-110
- Uplifting: 100-125

### Key Suggestions by Mood
- Warm/Cozy: C major, F major, G major
- Bittersweet: Am, Dm, Em
- Dreamy: Eb major, Ab major, Db major
- Mysterious: Cm, Bbm, F#m
- Nostalgic: D major, A major, E major
